Table Mountain

View on Map

Table Mountain is a stunning flat-topped mountain overlooking Cape Town, South Africa. Renowned for its breathtaking views, it is a part of the Table Mountain National Park and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visitors can hike up or take the cable car to the summit, where they can explore diverse flora and fauna, as well as enjoy panoramic vistas of the city, ocean, and surrounding mountains. The iconic mountain is also famous for its rich biodiversity, featuring over 1,400 plant species.

Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden

View on Map

Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden is one of the most celebrated botanical gardens in the world, located at the eastern foot of Table Mountain. Established in 1913, it showcases the unique flora of South Africa, with a focus on the Cape Floral Kingdom. The garden covers 528 hectares and features stunning walking trails, themed gardens, and seasonal exhibitions. Visitors can enjoy a variety of events, including outdoor concerts and guided tours, while soaking in the beauty of the surrounding natural landscape.

Boulders Beach

View on Map

Boulders Beach is famous for its unique colony of African penguins. Located near Simon's Town, this picturesque beach offers a mix of sandy shores and large granite boulders, creating a sheltered area perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Visitors can observe the playful penguins in their natural habitat from designated boardwalks, making it a family-friendly destination.
